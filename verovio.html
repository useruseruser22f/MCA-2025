Week 3

Here I compare three elements in my Cantabile encoding (MusicXML vs MEI): <note>, <measure>, and clefs. MusicXML uses nested child elements (e.g. <pitch>, <duration>), while MEI often stores the same information as attributes on <note> and <clef>. This makes MEI more compact and suited for metadata-rich, analytical work, whereas MusicXML is designed mainly for interchange between notation programs and playback.

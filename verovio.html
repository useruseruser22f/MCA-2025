Week 3

Here I compare three elements in my Cantabile encoding (MusicXML vs MEI): note, measure, and clefs. MusicXML uses more nested child elements for example, pitch and duration are stored in pitch and duration, while MEI encodes much of the same information as attributes on elements like note and clef. This makes MEI more compact and metadata-oriented, whereas MusicXML is designed primarily for interchange between notation programs and playback.
